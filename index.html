SAVE THE WORLD

REUSE, REDUCE & RECYCLE.

Triple R Revolution

As climate change tears through our current ecosystem there have been many efforts to mitigate its spread and impact; from sensitizing images of places stricking with its effects, to solutions to replace harmful processes that contribute to its spread.

However, to no avail all have proved unsustainable from the social responsibility aspect. Due to the structure of these solutions they have a strong rejection factor on peoples everyday activities as they are preoccupied with other tasks during the day, the topic of climate change ever crosses their mind, this is especially for the lives of common people who might never face such effects from the climate.

The key note in this problem is seen in the synchronisation of this solution into peoples everyday lives; and Triple R Revolution aims to solve this by fitting into a solution that involves interaction with the basic part of everyones lives, the social aspect; and in particular a social media platform that encourages the tenets of a sustainable world REUSE, REDUCE RECYCLE.

Our Objectives

Increase awareness of environmental issues: One of the primary objectives of the project should be to increase awareness of environmental issues.

Reduce waste and promote recycling: The project's focus on recycling, reusing, and reducing should also have the objective of reducing waste and promoting recycling.

Promote collaboration on environmental initiatives: The project's platform can bring people with similar environmental interests together.

Encourage sustainable lifestyle changes: By providing a platform for users to share their environmentally friendly activities, the project can inspire others to adopt similar behaviors...

Who we are!

Tiple R Revolution is a team of Software Engineering students based in African Leadership University who bears Environment Conservation as their mission. We aims to use Technology solution to combat the impact that the current Development is causing having on our Enviroment and our Climate
